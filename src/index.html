Who Are We

Arc Al-Bonian General Contracting was established with its great dream and endless limits in providing quality solutions and using the latest technology methods, advanced engineering techniques, and the most creative designs to enter the field of construction and development from its widest doors with its competitive advantages. Arc Al-Bonian believes that the whole world is moving at a rapid pace to a tremendous technological development and it must keep pace with this development and contribute effectively to serving our community and providing the best services with the highest standards of quality and professionalism because the entity has specialized technical and engineering cadres with long experience and trained labor according to scientific foundations and high efficiency, taking into account the highest safety standards to contribute seriously and effectively to construction and development, as well as aim to build a well-known name and a brilliant brand in its field.

Vision

Leading in providing the services of the contracting sector by working professionally and achieving the best achievements in all our fields and services provided to our valued customers, and that Arc Al-Bonian Contracting is a leading Saudi Company in the field of its services, and striving to be one of the advanced pillars of the highest status and the best performance in the field of contracting, glass, and other services we provide.

Arc Al-Bonian Contracting seeks to dedicate its efforts to building honest and long-term relationships with its customers and partners and employing the best and latest materials, solutions, and technologies to provide high-quality services that are keen on the satisfaction of all its customers and meet their ambitions with the constant pursuit of continuous development and modernization to serve this great country.
